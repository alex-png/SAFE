Why SAFE?

Here at SAFE, we understand the obstacle of saving money in a world where spending is so easy. SAFE is designed to serve as a free online piggy bank for people saving for unique financial goals. You decide how much and how often you want to contribute and through Wayfair's 3D Furniture Model progress is tracked based on retrieving virtual furniture to complete a "full" home. However, if you remove money from your savings account- you'll have to start over again. As an incentive, users are discouraged to remove money because they also have a risk of paying 3 percent of their goal to SAFE. We want our users to SAVE for their GOALS!

SAFE delivers a focused driven solution and opportunity to grow this already existing network of saving money. The difference? No interest.

Build Your Home

Keep building your home everyday, every single furniture represents how much you add to your savings account.

Plus, you earn rewards points which can be redeemed for gift gards!
